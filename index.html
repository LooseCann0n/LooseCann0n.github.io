Welcome to my Portfolio


Greetings! I'm Thomas Billett, a graduate of Falmouth University with a passion for making games. Ever since my first encounter with video games, I have been driven to recreate that enchanting feeling that engrossed my fascination. My journey through the Computing for Games course at Falmouth not only equipped me with a solid foundation with the technical skills necessary to create games but also emphasized the importance of effective teamwork.
Collaborating with my peers from diverse disciplines within the game development process has been a fundamental part of my degree, fostering my ability to seamlessly work alongside individuals with varying skill sets. My proficiency lies in C#, and I also possess a firm understanding of C++, leveraging these skills within the dynamic environments of Unity and Unreal Engine.
Furthermore, my experience extends to utilizing Git for version control and strong understanding of the agile development methodology. Ensuring the seamless management and coordination of collaborative projects. I am eager to channel my skills and passion into contributing to innovative and captivating game development projects.
